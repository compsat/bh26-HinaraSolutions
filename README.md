## The Problem

Many Filipino households experience **“Meralco anxiety”**, where high electricity bills arrive 30 days after usage.  

- People blast their ACs during summer with **zero visibility** into their real-time electricity consumption.  
- This leads to **bill shock**, financial stress, and inefficient energy habits.  

---

## Solution & Product Overview

WattZap is a **predictive energy dashboard** that allows users to:  

- Input appliance types (e.g., “1.5 HP Inverter AC”) and daily usage habits.  
- See a live **“Burn Rate”** of electricity spending against their budget.  
- Receive **AI-driven insights** on how to optimize usage and save.  
- Gamify energy savings through **community leaderboards** and **mini-challenges**.  

Users don’t need expensive smart meters—just their appliances and usage patterns.  

---

## Key Features

- **Live Burn Rate:** Monitor real-time energy usage vs. budget.  
- **Predictive ML Model:** Learns home-specific thermal dynamics and usage patterns.  
- **AI Insights:** Personalized recommendations for energy savings.  
- **Dynamic Rate Updates:** Automatic updates of Meralco rates via scraping.  
- **Voice-Assisted Input:** Quick logging of daily appliance usage.  
- **Gamification:** Badges, points, and community challenges.  

---

## Technology & Novelty

- **ML Model / Q-Learning:** Predicts energy consumption based on appliances, usage, and thermal behavior.  
- **Weather API Integration:** Adjusts predictions based on live and forecasted weather.  
- **Next.js Frontend:** Sleek, interactive, real-time dashboard.  
- **AI Tips & Alerts:** Guides users to optimize usage, e.g., “Switch AC to fan mode to stay under ₱4,000.”  

---

## User Flow

1. **Login & Sign-Up:** Save appliance data and preferences.  
2. **Cartoon Dashboard:** Playful mascot gives tips, encouragement, and alerts.  
3. **Appliance Input:** Add type, power, quantity, usage status, and daily hours (with default values).  
4. **Analytics & Feedback:** View estimated monthly costs, “what-if” scenarios, and AI tips.  
5. **Voice Input:** Log usage via voice commands like:  
   - “Today I used the AC for 6 hours.”  
6. **Dynamic Rate Updates:** Keep estimates accurate with current Meralco kWh rates.  

---

## Gamification & Engagement

- **Community Leaderboards:** Compare savings with friends, family, or neighborhoods.  
- **Mini-Challenges:** Encourage daily or weekly energy-efficient habits.  
- **Badges & Milestones:** Reward energy-saving achievements.  

---

## Main Tabs & Navigation

1. **Dashboard (Home):** Live Burn Rate, tips from mascot, daily vs. budget overview.  
2. **Appliances / Usage:** Add, edit, remove appliances; view consumption stats.  
3. **Analytics & Insights:** AI-driven predictions, graphs, “what-if” scenarios.  
4. **Community / Leaderboards:** Track friends, local teams, energy milestones.  
5. **Settings / Profile:** Manage account, budget, notifications, and voice assistant.  
6. **Help / Tips:** Quick guides, FAQs, and mini-tutorials from mascot.
